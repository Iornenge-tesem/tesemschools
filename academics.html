Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum follows the Nigerian national standards while incorporating modern, learner-centred approaches.

At Tesem Model Schools, we deliver a comprehensive curriculum approved by the Federal Ministry of Education and aligned with NERDC guidelines. Our programme covers nursery, primary, and secondary levels, with each stage carefully structured to build upon the previous one.

We emphasise literacy, numeracy, sciences, and social studies, while also integrating ICT, creative arts, and moral instruction into everyday learning. Our curriculum is regularly reviewed to reflect current educational best practices and to prepare students for national examinations such as Common Entrance, BECE, WAEC, NECO, and JAMB.
