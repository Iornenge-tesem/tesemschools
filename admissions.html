Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to secure a place for your child.

1

Obtain the Application Form

Visit our school office or click the "Apply Now" button below to request an application form. A non-refundable fee applies.

2

Complete & Submit the Form

Fill out all sections of the form accurately and attach the required documents. Submit the form at the school front desk.

3

Entrance Assessment

Candidates will take a short assessment in English and Mathematics (not applicable for nursery). Parents will be notified of the schedule.

4

Parent / Guardian Interview

A brief meeting with the school management to discuss your child's needs and to share school expectations.

5

Admission Offer

Successful candidates will receive an admission letter. Parents should confirm acceptance and complete fee payment within the specified period.

6

Resumption

Your child is officially enrolled! Collect the school package (uniform, books, and materials list) and prepare for the first day of school.
